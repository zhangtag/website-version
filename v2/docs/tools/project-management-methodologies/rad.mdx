<p className="grey">A more adaptive approach to software development</p>

While regular plan-based methods require a rigid structure with specific requirements, RAD is based around flexibility to adapt alongside new knowledge. 

RAD emphasises the use of feedback to change and adapt. 

This method is unlike Waterfall.
